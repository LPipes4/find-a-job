Job Match

Job Match is a service that allows employers and job seekers to be matched based on their respective needs and profiles.

Resume Builder

Youth and student job training

Government of Canada programs to help youth and students get the skills they need to start their careers.

Job Bank postings for students and youth

Up to date list of jobs that are available for students and youth on Job Bank.

Research Affiliate Program

Provides post-secondary students with opportunities to conduct innovative research and gain experience with federal organizations.

Federal Student Work Experience Program (FSWEP)

The Federal Student Work Experience Program (FSWEP) helps match students with federal departments and agencies for temporary jobs.

National Research Council Summer Employment program

The National Research Council Canada (NRC) Summer Employment Program provides post-secondary students with practical work experience in research and development, library sciences, communications and marketing.

National Research Council Co-op Program

The National Research Council Co-op Program offers work terms to post-secondary students so that they can acquire practical and professional work experience.

Career Focus Program

The Career Focus program offers youth a range of work experiences, learning and skill-building activities to help them choose careers and to encourage them to pursue advanced studies.

Canadian Coast Guard careers

Explore exciting and challenging job opportunities for youth and new grads. Discover fast-paced, satisfying careers on land and at sea.

Legal Excellence Program

The Legal Excellence Program provides employment opportunities to law students, articling law students, and junior lawyers and notaries.
